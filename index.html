Frequently Asked Questions

Why is background in biology and toxicology a differentiator?

In drug cases, DUI, and healthcare fraud, government evidence is scientific. Saam has the technical knowledge to identify whether a lab test was mishandled or if the science applied is flawed—something attorneys without this background cannot challenge effectively.

What does it mean to have a former prosecutor on my defense?

It means your attorney knows the inner workings of the system. He understands how prosecutors think, how they prioritize cases, and most importantly, where they typically fail in building their evidence.

Does Saam Zangeneh handle cases outside Florida?

Yes. At the federal level, he is licensed to represent clients in all 50 U.S. States, as well as Puerto Rico and the U.S. Virgin Islands.
